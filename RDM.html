Demo for Random Dot Motion Task

This is a demo for the Random Dot Motion Task. I believe that in order to pursue truth in science, researchers should share experimental code when possible in order to further exploration in psychology. To that end, here is a demonstration for code that I have created to administer the Random Dot Motion Task online. This code uses the paper.js library in conjuction with jquery.js library.

For this task you will be asked to indicate whether the dots on your screen are moving LEFT or RIGHT.

As you will see all the dots are moving randomly and a select group of them are moving either left or right. Your job is to decide whether the dots are moving left or right.
