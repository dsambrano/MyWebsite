Hi, I am Deshawn Sambrano. I am a Data Scientist/Applied Scientist with a passion for solving problems with data. I received my PhD from Harvard where I studied Economics, Psychology, and Neuroscience. My previous work has focused on modeling consumer choices and designing A/B tests to modify their decisions.

Since 2022, I have been consulting for Activision on the world renowned Call of Duty Franchise. There, I was able to continue developing strategies to modify consumers behaviors in a production contexts going out to 10s of millions of players world wide!
